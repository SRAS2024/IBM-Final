About In tuned

Description:

A simple reflection tool that lets you write current thoughts to help you process how you feel in the moment. Use it as a helper, not as advice.

How it works:

Your text is analyzed across seven core emotions on a continuous scale. We select a dominant core and also compute a final two emotion label that best matches the mixture. The goal is clarity and self reflection.
