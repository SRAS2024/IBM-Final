About In tuned

Description:

A reflection tool to help you process how you feel in the moment. Write your thoughts; treat the output as a prompt for self-awareness, not advice.

How it works:

Your text is analyzed across seven core emotions. We show the dominant core and a single blended label that best fits the mixture.
